Gentle Reset Workout Plan (2x5 Minutes Daily)

3 Days a Week • Morning & Evening • Total: 10 Minutes/Day

Day 1 (Monday)

Morning Session:

  • 1 min: March in Place (Gentle Warm-Up)
  • 1 min: Arm Circles + Shoulder Rolls
  • 1 min: Step Jacks or Side Steps
  • 1 min: High Knees (march if needed)
  • 1 min: Stretch (Hamstrings, Quads, Shoulders)

Evening Session:

  • 1 min: Light Jog or Walk in Place
  • 1 min: Squat to Reach (or sit to stand)
  • 1 min: Arm Swings + Cross Body Punches
  • 1 min: Standing Side Crunch
  • 1 min: Cool Down Stretch

Day 2 (Wednesday)

Morning Session:

  • 1 min: March in Place (Gentle Warm-Up)
  • 1 min: Side Steps or Step Jacks
  • 1 min: High Knees or Knee Lifts
  • 1 min: Shoulder Rolls + Arm Swings
  • 1 min: Stretch (Hamstrings, Back, Arms)

Evening Session:

  • 1 min: Walk in Place + Deep Breathing
  • 1 min: Seated or Standing Squats
  • 1 min: Step Touch with Arm Swings
  • 1 min: Wall Push-ups or Air Punches
  • 1 min: Gentle Cool Down Stretch

Day 3 (Friday)

Morning Session:

  • 1 min: March in Place (Gentle Warm-Up)
  • 1 min: Step Backs or Light Lunges
  • 1 min: Arm Circles + Shoulder Rolls
  • 1 min: High Knees (march if needed)
  • 1 min: Stretch (Neck, Arms, Legs)

Evening Session:

  • 1 min: Walk in Place
  • 1 min: Gentle Squats or Sit to Stand
  • 1 min: Step Side to Side
  • 1 min: Arm Raises with Breathing
  • 1 min: Full Body Stretch
